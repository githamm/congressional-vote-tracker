Colorado congressional vote tracker

Curious about how your U.S. congressional representative is voting, and what they're voting on? The colored circles indicate how a representative voted on a resolution (NV stands for "not voting"). If a bill is listed more than once, the earlier votes are often motions, such as a motion to table — the most recent result is the final vote. Click the date for more information on a bill. Some bill titles are really long — click the "+" button to see the full title (or hover over the title). The tracker will be updated daily.
